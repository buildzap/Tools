About Keyword Density

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears in your content compared to the total number of words.

Features:
  • Word count analysis
  • Custom stop words
  • Case sensitivity option
  • Density recommendations
Tip: Aim for a keyword density between 1-3% for optimal SEO results.
