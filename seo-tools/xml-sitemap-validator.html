About Sitemap Validation

XML sitemaps help search engines understand your website structure. Validating your sitemap ensures it follows the correct format and contains valid URLs.

Checks:
  • XML Format
  • URL Validity
  • Required Tags
  • Priority Values
Tip: Keep your sitemap under 50MB and 50,000 URLs for optimal performance.
