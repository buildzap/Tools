About Energy Converter

Convert between different energy units including Joules (J), Kilojoules (kJ), Calories (cal), Kilocalories (kcal), Watt-hours (Wh), Kilowatt-hours (kWh), and Electron Volts (eV).

Features:
  • Multiple units
  • Real-time conversion
  • Common conversions
  • Conversion history
Tip: Use the swap button to quickly switch between units.
