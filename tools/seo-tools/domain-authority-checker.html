About Domain Authority

Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score that predicts how likely a website is to rank in search results.

Metrics:
  • Domain Authority (DA)
  • Page Authority (PA)
  • Backlink count
  • Quality score
Tip: Higher DA scores indicate better ranking potential.
